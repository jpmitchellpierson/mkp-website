About Michael Pierson

Experience

Since 1976, Michael Pierson has been developing timeless, one-of-a-kind custom properties that have commanded top sales figures in each market in which he has built. Over his 40+ years of experience, Michael has formed a keen insight into the luxury real estate market. He intimately understands the subtle nuances of market opportunity.

Artistry

Michael's projects embody his belief that a truly elegant, classic and pure design will remain so forever. His philosophy is to maintain integrity in all disciplines, from design,construction and site selection to furnishings and artwork.

Inspired by nature and history, Michael integrates the architectural style of each geographic locale with state-of-the-art technology to create an environment that blends warm and intimate spaces with sophistication and unsurpassed quality. Sensitivity to scale is critical, and directly related to the sense of well-being one experiences upon entering a space. Comfort should never be compromised.

Expertise

As a highly skilled executive with more than 40 years of experience in luxury real estate development, Michael has a proven track record successfully developing and implementing business strategy, driving growth and profitability, marketing new business ventures, securing investors, and building some of the most beautiful homes in the world.
